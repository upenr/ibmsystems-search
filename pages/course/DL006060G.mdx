# Short Summary
This course explores topics focused on new and interesting features of z/OS in 2024 at a higher level designed to take early tenured users to the next level.

# Duration in Hours ⌚
16.0

# Content Description
By completing this in-person course, passing the topic specific quizzes and passing the final exam, students will be eligible to earn the z/OS Academy at IBM TechXchange badge. Earners are expected to have a basic understanding of the topics as well as where to find more information to continue learning and growing their proficiency.

# Objectives
This badge earner has demonstrated an understanding of the latest skills, processes, and tools needed to efficiently and effectively manage z/OS systems operations. The earner is equipped with the knowledge to discuss and leverage technologies such as z/OS Management Facility (z/OSMF), z/OS Containers, z/OS Workload Manager (WLM), and Machine Learning and AI Frameworks for z/OS to optimize their workloads and unleash new capabilities.
