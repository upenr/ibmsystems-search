# Content Description
<p>This 2 day course provides students with the skills needed to implement and manager the IBM VM Recovery Manager â€“ HA (VMR-HA) product.&nbsp; Topics include introduction to High Availability (HA) and Disaster Recovery (DR), understanding the requirements and planning for implementing VMR-HA, installing and configuring VMR-HA, and testing the capabilities of VMR-HA at the VM and application level. Hands-on exercises reinforce the lecture material, allowing students to install, configure, test, and maintain VMR-HA environments.</p>
