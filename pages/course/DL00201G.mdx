# Short Summary
Attend three Z Tech Bytes required sessions and pass the quiz to earn the "IBM Z Tech Bytes - November 2024 - WebSphere Liberty" badge.

# Duration in Hours ⌚
4.0

# Content Description
To earn the "IBM Z Tech Bytes - November 2024 - WebSphere Liberty" badge, participants must attend three required sessions, covering key topics including Monitoring and Problem Determination Techniques, Configuration Best Practices, and Understanding the Security Options. After attending or watching the replays, you'll take a quiz to test your understanding of the concepts presented. Successful completion of both requirements will earn you a digital badge to showcase your technical skills in the area of WebSphere Liberty.

# Objectives
This badge earner has improved their technical skills in the area of WebSphere Liberty on IBM Z. They have learned from IBM Z subject matter experts through a series of presentations and demonstrations to grow and expand their technical skills. They have also demonstrated that they can apply these technical skills going forward in their IBM Z organization and career.
