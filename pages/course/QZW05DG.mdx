# Content Description
<p>&nbsp;This course covers the steps to follow to update a Red Hat OpenShift Container Platform 4.3.18 environment on Power Systems to 4.3.23. &nbsp;Lessons learned, and hints and tips will be provided to help you perform the update successfully.</p>

# Objectives
<p>After completing this course, you should be able to:</p><ul><li>Identify the Red Hat OpenShift support timeline (as of course publish date)</li><li>Utilize upgrade channels as the source for the upgrade operation</li><li>Update a cluster using various tools</li><li>Monitor the status and success or failure of the update process</li></ul>

# Agenda
<ul><li>Red Hat OpenShift support timeline</li><li>Upgrade channels - Fast and Stable</li><li>Choosing the proper channel for upgrade</li><li>Tools to upgrade the cluster</li><li>Monitor the upgrade progress</li></ul>

# Audience
