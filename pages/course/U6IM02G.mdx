# Content Description
The dynamic of this course is valuable for any storage professional that needs to work with the IBM FlashSystem 5000, 7200 or 9200 (formerly IBM Storwize V5000E or V7200) of who is a member of the FlashSystem family of storage solutions. Start with required background knowledge enabling newer technicians to understand fundamentals storage concepts.
