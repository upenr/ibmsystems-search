# Short Summary
Attend three Z Tech Bytes required sessions and pass the quiz to earn the "IBM Z Tech Bytes - November 2024 - z/OS Performance and Technical Updates" badge.

# Duration in Hours ⌚
4.0

# Content Description
To earn the "IBM Z Tech Bytes - November 2024 - z/OS Performance and Technical Updates" badge, participants must attend three required sessions, covering key topics including WSC z/OS Performance ‘Hot Topics’, Optimize the Batch Window with the Latest IBM Z Technology - zBNA 2024 Update, and z16 Performance Considerations and Best Practices. After attending or watching the replays, you'll take a quiz to test your understanding of the concepts presented. Successful completion of both requirements will earn you a digital badge to showcase your technical skills in the area of z/OS Performance and Technical Updates.

# Objectives
This badge earner has improved their technical skills in the area of z/OS Performance and Technical Updates. They have learned from IBM Z subject matter experts through a series of presentations and demonstrations to grow and expand their technical skills. They have also demonstrated that they can apply these technical skills going forward in their IBM Z organization and career.
