# Short Summary
Attend three Z Tech Bytes required sessions and pass the quiz to earn the "IBM Z Tech Bytes - November 2024 - Hybrid Cloud and AI" badge.

# Duration in Hours ⌚
4.0

# Content Description
To earn the "IBM Z Tech Bytes - November 2024 - Hybrid Cloud and AI" badge, participants must attend three required sessions, covering key topics including Ansible with z/OS, AIOps with IBM Z, and Explainable AI with Machine Learning for z/OS. After attending or watching the replays, you'll take a quiz to test your understanding of the concepts presented. Successful completion of both requirements will earn you a digital badge to showcase your technical skills in the area of Hybrid Cloud & AI on IBM Z and LinuxONE.

# Objectives
This badge earner has improved their technical skills in the area of Hybrid Cloud on IBM Z and LinuxONE. They have learned from IBM Z and LinuxONE subject matter experts through a series of presentations and demonstrations to grow and expand their technical skills. They have also demonstrated that they can apply these technical skills going forward in their IBM Z organization and career.
