# Content Description
<p>This course is designed to help you understand, install, and monitor the DS8000 for the Open Systems environment. The exercises enable you to perform a step-by-step implementation.</p>

# Objectives
<p>&bull; Recall the DS8000 models and features<br />
&bull; Remember the architecture and hardware associated with the DS8000<br />
&bull; Classify the DS8000 virtualization concepts<br />
&bull; Summarize the DS8000 DS8000 command-line interface<br />
&bull; Summarize the DS8000 graphical user interface<br />
&bull; List the Copy Services features<br />
&bull; Classify the features associated with performance, tuning, and monitoring<br />
&bull; List the steps required to configure the DS8000 for Open Systems environment<br />
&bull; Recognize the IBM DS8000 solutions for Business Continuity</p>
