# Content Description
<p>This course is designed to enable the students to acquire the skills necessary to prepare for installing and leveraging the new functions of IBM i 7.3 on POWER7 and POWER8 hardware.</p>

<p>The emphasis is on the hardware and software components introduced in 2015-2016 announcements of POWER8 hardware and the 2016 announcement of IBM i 7.3.</p>

# Objectives
<p>After completing this course, you should be able to:</p>

<p>&bull; Describe the new models of POWER8</p>

<p>&bull; Describe the significant POWER8 hardware</p>

<p>&bull; Describe single root IO virtualization</p>

<p>&bull; Describe the new enhancements to IBM i 7.3 software release</p>

<p>&bull; Describe the major planning steps to upgrade to IBM i 7.3</p>

<p>&bull; Describe the major enhancements of DB2</p>

<p>&bull; Describe the major security enhancements</p>

<p>&bull; Describe the enhancements to IBM i Access</p>

<p>&bull; Describe the enhancements of IBM Navigator for i</p>

<p>&bull; Describe the enhancements for application development</p>

<p>&bull; Describe the enhancements to Open Source for IBM i</p>

<p>&bull; Describe the enhancements IBM i mobile</p>

<p>&bull; Describe PowerHA/DR strategy and product offerings</p>

<p>&bull; Describe the major functions of PowerVC</p>

<p>&bull; Describe the major functions of PowerVP</p>
