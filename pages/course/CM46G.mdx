# Content Description
<p>Learn about the Information Management System (IMS) High Availability Large Database (HALDB). Examine how databases may be migrated to HALDB and the advantages of using HALDB.</p>

# Objectives
<ul>
	<li>Define HALDB databases and their partitions</li>
	<li>Understand database administrator responsibilities in managing HALDB</li>
	<li>Develop procedures for the support and recovery of HALDB</li>
	<li>Migrate databases to the use of HALDB</li>
	<li>Understand application design for HALDB</li>
</ul>

# Agenda
<ul><li>Overview</li><li>Database Structures</li><li>Definition</li><li>Partition Selection</li><li>DBRC Support</li><li>Database Commands</li><li>Utilities</li><li>Database Creation and Migration</li><li>Changing Databases</li><li>Secondary Indexes</li><li>Application and Miscellaneous Considerations</li><li>HALDB Online Reorganization</li><li>Summary</li></ul>
