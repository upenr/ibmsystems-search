# Content Description
<p>Learn about the Information Management System (IMS) High Availability Large Database (HALDB). Examine how databases may be migrated to HALDB and the advantages of using HALDB.</p>

# Objectives
<ul />
	<li />Define HALDB databases and their partitions
	<li />Understand database administrator responsibilities in managing HALDB
	<li />Develop procedures for the support and recovery of HALDB
	<li />Migrate databases to the use of HALDB
	<li />Understand application design for HALDB
